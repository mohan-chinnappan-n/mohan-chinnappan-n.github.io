Respect the creators

  • Nature is the greatest Creator and the Artist combined
  • Understanding and respecting the Nature is the foundation principle of life
  • We humans do not have an ability to create even an unicellular Amoeba - so we do NOT have any rights to destroy any life created by the Nature
  • Nature has a goal for every life it creates. Understanding that goal forms the soul-searching for the Nature's creations
  • Live like trees! Trees talk to each other - share water/nutrients and communicate through networks
  • Be a creator and a helper like the Nature by practicing and celebrating professions like Farming, Wood Working...
  • Respect and celebrate other's creations. We stand on the shoulders of many other creators

Love is the universal Binder

  • Love amplifies the life while hate diminishes the life
  • People ignorant of Love start Wars and bring destruction to the Nature's creations, reverse the life's progress-clock and we see those people vanish from the world in an non-illuminated way
  • Always remember our design goal written by the Nature. Nothing should differentiate and divide us!. Bind the lives/creations with Love!
  • Harness the power of Love by viewing positives in all the creations of the Nature

Knowledge is the only Lasting Wealth

  • Progress of a human kind is measured by number of inventions and quality of knowledge base created
  • If the Nature does any destruction, we can bring back our creations with the knowledge base

Share the Wealth/Knowledge in a scalable-organic way

  • Teach the noble trades like Farming, but do not provide free food - this will deviate from the Nature's design goals
  • Nature shares everything with us. We can get them if we work to get them. Sun burns everyday to feed us

Timely Help

  • Look for helping the Nature's creations at the time-of-need
  • காலத்தி னாற்செய்த நன்றி சிறிதெனினும்

    A timely benefit, - though thing of little worth, The gift itself, - in excellence transcends the earth.

  • War is for ignorant people whose wisdom eyes/ears are closed
  • அறிவற்றங் காக்குங் கருவி செறுவார்க்கும்
