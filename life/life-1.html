Respect the creators

  • Nature is the greatest Creator and the Artist combined
  • Understanding and respecting the Nature is the foundation principle of life
  • We humans do not have an ability to create even unicellular Amoeba - so we do NOT have any rights to destroy any life created by Nature
  • Nature has a goal for every life it creates. Understanding that goal forms the soul-searching for the Nature's creations
  • Trees talk to each other - share water/nutrients and communicate through networks. Live like trees!
  • Be a creator and a helper like Nature by practicing professions like Farming, Wood Working...
  • Respect and celebrate other's creations. We stand on the shoulders of many other creators

Love is the universal Binder

  • Love amplifies the life - hate diminishes the life
  • People ignorant of Love start Wars and bring destruction to Nature's creation, reverse the progress-clock and those people vanish from the world in a non-illuminated way
  • Always remember our design goal written by Nature. Nothing should differentiate and divide us!. Bind the lives with Love!
  • Harness the power of Love by viewing positives in all the creations of Nature

Knowledge is the only Lasting Wealth

  • Progress of a human kind is measured number of inventions and quality of knowledge base created
  • If Nature does any destruction (creator can do the destruction!), we can bring back our creations with the knowledge base

Share the Wealth/Knowledge in a scalable-organic way

  • Natures shares everything with us. Sun burns everyday to feed us
