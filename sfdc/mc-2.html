Features of MC

  • Connect to your consumers where they are (for example, email, mobile, web).
  • Offer tailored recommendations, reminders, and discounts.
  • Listen to what consumers say on social media and respond to their needs, including providing real-time customer service.

MC Products

  • DATA: Put consumer data at the center of everything you do.
    • Salesforce DMP: Capture, unify, and activate data in the leading data management platform. Whether you’re a marketer or a media owner, use data at scale to personalize every consumer touchpoint.
    • Data Studio: Develop new audiences and build new partnerships via peer-to-peer data sharing , and use that data to enhance existing campaigns.
    • Interaction Studio: Automatically reconcile consumer data across owned online and offline marketing touchpoints in this real-time interaction management platform. Provide real-time context for recommended next actions on each channel and device.
    • Journey Builder: Plan, build, and automate consumer engagement at scale across channels and devices. Drive personalization throughout the consumer lifecycle automatically.
  • TOOLS: Engage with consumers when, where, and how they want using proven engagement tools.
    • Email Studio: Engage consumers with tailored, personalized messages with speed, security, scalability, and excellent deliverability.
    • Mobile Studio: Align SMS marketing, push notifications, and group messaging to your brand’s overall marketing strategy, and ensure consumers are reached at the right moment based on events, location, or proximity.
    • Advertising Studio : Activate customer data across Salesforce to power personalized advertising across social, search, and display.
    • Social Studio: Integrate the power of social media messaging with service and sales to listen, engage, and analyze consumer interactions and sentiment in real time.

MC Connect

  • Integrates MC across the Customer Success Platform, giving you the ability to deliver connected consumer engagement across your entire business.

Messaging Products

Email Studio

  • Send Intelligent and Personalized email marketing at scale
  • Segment, automate, and target your customers based on the data you collect from them
  • Track every email send and open rate, so you know exactly how your emails are performing.

Email Studio - contd.

  • Use drag-and-drop audience segmentation tools
  • Scale and automate all interactions with your customers
  • Create dynamic email content with drag-and-drop tools.
  • Use mobile-optimized email templates.
  • Examples:
    1. Create and send a “happy birthday” email to your subscribers when it’s their birthday with a celebratory 20% off coupon.
    2. Use Email Studio for mass communications to your employees about an event or organizational change.

Mobile Studio

  • Reach customers at the right time, with the right message, regardless of location.
  • Increase your immediate customer’s engagement by sending SMS, MMS, and push messaging based on events, location, and proximity.
  • Use mobile APIs to create and automate custom messages, such as daily appointment alerts, order confirmations, and travel updates.
  • Integrate your CRM data and marketing analytics with your mobile campaigns to have real-time interactions with your customers.

Mobile Studio - contd.

  • Track and send text messages with SMS.
  • Have two-way mobile conversations that include pictures, videos, and audio with MMS, and group messaging.
  • Increase use of your app with push messaging.
  • Use automated geofencing (interactions based on your customer’s GPS location).
  • Track when customers open your app, how much time they spend interacting with it, and details about their location
  • Create and automate custom solutions with mobile APIs.
  • Use ready-made SMS and MMS templates and drag-and-drop tools to customize your messages.
  • Examples:
    1. Send your customers mobile tickets and coupons to complete customer services updates and surveys. Keep your customers engaged with your brand and drive customer loyalty.
    2. Connect the physical world with the digital world, and when a customer walks into one of your physical stores, send them a push message with the latest deals. This helps you engage with your customers right when they’re ready to take the kind of action you’re trying to drive.

Journey Builder

  • Create a coordinated customer journey at scale across email, mobile, and advertising, and triggers based on customer behavior, preferences, or other data points.
  • Customize journeys to respond to customer purchases, clicks, or email opens, and define the path that the customer takes based on their current and predicted behavior./li>
  • Map the customer journey, and adjust the customer’s path based on their behavior.
  • Use data from any source
  • Test multiple variations of content, channel, timing, and frequency.

Journey Builder - Example Use case

  • Build a customer’s journey for your loyalty program marketing campaign.
  • Using the drag-and-drop tools in Journey Builder, you create the flow of how and when emails and push notifications are sent to your subscribers.
  • You add a decision split into the journey:
    • When the customer joins the loyalty program so it sends the new loyalty member a coupon.
    • If the customer doesn’t join the loyalty program in the time frame you define, then a reminder email is sent to reengage the customer.

Advertising Studio - Usecase Examples

  • You want to reengage customers who haven’t opened or clicked your emails for 180 days. First, you use data from your CRM to identify those subscribers, then target them with advertising based on their CRM data—inactive customers become active again.
  • You use your customer’s purchase history to tailor the ads that appear in their web browser. These upsell ads include customized special offers specifically tailored for each customer, based on products they’ve purchased in the past 30 days. By connecting your CRM customer data with your marketing campaigns, you not only make your customers happy ☺ but also make your marketing efforts more effective.

Salesforce Data Studio

  • Data sharing platform for marketers, brands, and publishers
  • Allows marketers to expand their reach, discover new audiences, and supercharge their DMP
  • Brands and publishers use it to safely share and provision their owned data with secure permissions to drive incremental revenue
  • Use cases: win-win:
    1. Marketers are always looking to acquire new customers and often want access to data that publishers own. Historically, publishers and data owners have been reluctant to share their data because they were unable to control what was shared and with whom. Data Studio solves both problems in a secure environment that is fully controlled by data owners, so data buyers and data sellers can transact with confidence. Marketers connect with bigger and more targeted audiences and publishers get paid for controlled access to their data. Can you say win-win

Social Media Management Product

Social Studio

  • Can listen, publish, engage, and analyze your customer’s conversations on social media.
  • Helps you to listen to the social media conversation about your brand, then identify the most engaging content.
  • Share your analysis with different product, regional, or brand teams.

Social Studio - use cases

  1. Your service social team discovers a customer posted on Twitter about shipment problem - that they didn’t receive the right item with their order. This triggers your services social team to take immediate action and respond to the customer on Twitter and make the order right. Your customer is impressed with the immediate response and thanks you on Twitter by recommending your company to their followers. With Social Studio you improve response time and increase customer satisfaction rates.
  2. A member of your sales team uses social listening to learn about someone interested in your company's products. Your sales team immediately responds to that lead with relevant information, and now has an audit trail on the lead. Connecting Social Studio to the Sales Cloud helps social teams connect sales teams with qualified leads.
