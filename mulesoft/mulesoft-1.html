Integration Cloud Features

  • Helps Companies connect Applications, Data and Devices to make them pluggable and reusable
  • No more rigid point-to-point connections. Build enduring applications network that allows reuse and self-service
  • Network that Bends not Brakes as your business grows!
  • Platform combines full API Life Cycle Development & Management with Enterprise-grade Connectivity
  • Allows to design APIs for your services and validate them before you build them
  • Build, Edit, Test and Debug Integrations graphically in an intuitive drag-and-drop environment
  • Easily connect to any data-source on-prem-systems, mainframes, databases, ERPs, SaaS apps, mobile apps and IoT devices
  • Exchange and build your services with pre-built connectors
  • Compose an integration-flow with pre-built template
  • Visually map your data with real-time data previews
  • Securely manage your APIs and operate your services in private or public clouds
  • The runtime engine combines real-time application integration and orchestration
  • You can deploy your integrations on-prem, in the cloud, or both - in real-time or in bulk-on-schedule
  • Manage it from a single web-interface
  • Control users & traffic, get a complete overview of the data moving across your enterprise
  • Enable teams to Discover, Self-Service, Build-Once and Reuse services, so that can innovate faster!
  • Secure your data in an environment that exceeds the industry's highest security standards!
