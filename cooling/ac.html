Science of Sweating - drinking lot of water

  • Each a drop of sweat that will evaporate taking a bit of your body heat away ( Science of sweat )
  • sweat helps cool you down two ways:
    • makes your skin feel cooler when it’s wet
    • when it evaporates it removes some heat (like Refrigerants)
      In a place with high humidity , there’re already lots of water molecules in the air.
      So your sweat won’t evaporate very much because the humid air can’t hold very many more water molecules.
      In drier environment, your sweat has a better chance of evaporating quickly and taking away your body heat.
      As more sweat evaporates from your body, the air close to you becomes more humid, making it harder for your sweat to evaporate.
      A fan help here: When the fan blows the more humid air away from you so that your sweat could evaporate more quickly
  • Your body uses a lot of its water to make and release all the sweat it takes to cool you down. If you don’t replace that water you can become dehydrated. So Drink lot of water!
